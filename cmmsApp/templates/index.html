Power Cable Process

Power Cable Engineering-to-Delivery Process

From initial design to final delivery, we manage the complete power cable lifecycle with precision and accountability. Our process begins with detailed technical analysis and load assessment to ensure optimal sizing and construction for each voltage class. We prepare and confirm datasheets covering conductor materials, insulation and sheath systems, armour types, and accessories, ensuring compliance with AS/NZS and specific utility standards. Manufacturing is conducted under strict QA protocols with full production traceability, followed by routine and type testing — including insulation resistance, partial discharge, tensile, and high-voltage performance — to verify long-term reliability. Once approved, cables are packed on export-grade drums with moisture protection, labelled for traceability, and shipped with full certification and compliance documentation. Our engineering team supports site integration through installation guidance, testing, and commissioning to deliver a fully verified, operationally ready system.

View Projects

  1. Analysis & Design

    Review load profiles, installation routes, soil and ambient conditions, and system voltage to determine optimal LV/MV/HV construction and installation method (tray, duct, direct-burial, or overhead).

  2. Specification & Datasheet Confirmation

    Finalize conductor material & class, insulation/sheath type, armouring, drum length, and accessories. Confirm datasheets and compliance with AS/NZS and utility standards.

  3. Manufacturing & Quality Assurance

    Our cables are manufactured in accordance with approved datasheets and applicable standards. In-process quality control is carried out at each stage, including conductor formation, insulation/extrusion, armouring, and outer sheath application. The factory quality assurance system ensures full traceability of materials and production batches.

  4. Testing & Certification

    Conduct routine and type tests insulation resistance, high-voltage, partial discharge, tensile, and UV/flame tests with factory-issued AS/NZS-compliant certificates.

  5. Export Packing & Delivery

    Package on export-grade wooden drums with moisture barriers and lifting provisions. Provide all documents test reports, COC, MSDS, and export/shipping details.

  6. Installation Support & Commissioning

    Provide guidance on pulling tensions, bend radius, jointing, and pre-energization checks. Support final testing, inspection, and handover for energization.
